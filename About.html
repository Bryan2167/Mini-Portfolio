Praise be to The Fool! The Fool who does not belong to this era. The Mysterious Ruler above the gray fog. The King in Yellow and Black who wields good fortune. I pray for the power of mystery; I pray for the power of change; I pray for Your blessings. I pray for Your angelic embrace. Oh! Lord of Mysteries! Oh! King of Space-Time! Your benevolence extends over the spirit world. I pray for Your blessings; I pray for Your loving Grace. I pray that You bless these lost lambs and guide them along the right path. May Your blessings lead them away from the path of evil. I shall be adding all the angels and subsidiary gods with each passing day. I hope all these oddball bards realize the right and pure path of life and turn away from the Mother's path of evil. Praise be to The Fool! I praise You, Lord of Mysteries! May The Fool bless us!
